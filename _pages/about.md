<p style="text-align:justify; text-justify:inter-ideograph;">
Hello! My name is Zhiyi Xiang. I was born in <a class='colorchangeonly' href="https://www.hnsn.gov.cn/hnsn/xzjsn/zjsn_newxx.shtml" target="_blank">Suining County</a>, Shaoyang City, Hunan Province, and grew up in <a class='colorchangeonly' href="https://www.hnloudi.gov.cn/loudi/zjld/zjld.shtml" target="_blank">Loudi City</a>, Hunan Province. I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Optical Engineering at the College of Advanced Interdisciplinary Studies, <a class='colorchangeonly' href="https://www.nudt.edu.cn/" target="_blank">National University of Defense Technology</a>, under the supervision of Prof. Shilong Jin and Associate Prof. Jian Zhou at the Laser Gyro Research Institute.
<br>
<br>
My research interests include <a class='colorchangeonly' href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laser_Doppler_velocimetry" target="_blank">Laser Doppler Velocimetry</a>, <a class='colorchangeonly' href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inertial_navigation_system" target="_blank">Inertial Navigation Technology</a>, and <a class='colorchangeonly' href="https://link.springer.com/referenceworkentry/10.1007/978-981-10-6946-8_291" target="_blank">Integrated Navigation Technology</a>. I am passionate about exploring innovative applications of these technologies to advance the field of navigation.
<br>
<br>
Beyond my academic pursuits, I am an avid enthusiast of history and geography, with a particular interest in Chinese history. I also enjoy gaming, with favorites such as League of Legends, Naraka: Bladepoint, GTA V, Red Dead Redemption 2, The Witcher 3, Monster Hunter: World, and the Battlefield series. Additionally, I have spent over 200 hours immersed in Civilization VI and No Manâ€™s Sky.
